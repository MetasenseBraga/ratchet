Quick Answers

FAQ's

What types of businesses do you work with?

We work with a wide range of businesses, from small startups to large corporations, across various industries. Contact us so that we can see if there is synergy.

What products and services do you offer?

We offer various products and services under our brands, and via our channel partners in different countries.

Why are you based in Abu Dhabi?

Abu Dhabi Global Market (ADGM), a freezone in Abu Dhabi, offers arguably the most forward-thinking and progressive digital assets regime in the world. We chose the location primarily for this reason.

Where do you do business?

We are open to doing business in most countries, but from a practical standpoint we are best equipped to deal in regions where we are represented by our channel partners.

Is your company listed on any public exchanges?

No, we are privately owned and operated.
